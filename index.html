Our Brand Consulting practice covers every critical aspect of building and crafting brands - From Purpose to Proposition to Portfolio. Our proprietary BrandGenome framework captures every aspect of the brand that can and should drive business and communication. Through DigiT, this practice also helps and guides brands embarking on their digital transformation journey.

analysts, Digital strategists and Media specialists working in tandem with Brand
Strategists, Communication Planners and Consumer Behaviourists – also why we have
heavily invested in data and tools; arguably more than any other creative enterprise in this
